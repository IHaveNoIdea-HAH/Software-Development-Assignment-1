how to play

Crossword is a game where you are given descriptions a.k.a. clues of words to guess. Pick a clue and guess the word correctly and fill the grid. If you manage to guess every word within the guess limit, you win! If you run out of guesses, you lose. You can auto solve a clue but you get penalty points for that. You can auto solve the entire puzzle but you will get penalty points for any unsolved clues and lose the game. Good luck!
